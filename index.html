How Epark Works

1

Create Your Account

Sign up on our secure mobile app. Link your preferred payment method (credit card, digital wallet) in minutes. It's fast, easy, and secure.

2

Order Your NFC Tag

Receive your sleek, durable Epark NFC tag in the mail. Simply attach it to your car's windshield or dashboard.

3

Drive Into a Lot

When you enter a Epark-enabled parking lot, our sensors detect your NFC tag. The barrier lifts automatically.

4

Park & Pay Automatically

Park your car. When you exit, your NFC tag is scanned again, and the parking fee is automatically charged to your linked payment method.

5

View History & Manage

Access your complete parking history, receipts, and manage your account settings anytime through the Epark app.

6

Future Enhancements

Look forward to features like pre-booking, reserved spots, and loyalty rewards coming soon!
